Anita Thang

I am a UX Designer based in Toronto, Canada.

I found my love for designing and creating when I was a kid, but it wasn't until I graduated from university (Nutrition Major) that I fully transited into the UX Design field.

I'm a problem-solver whose strength is finding multiple ways to approach the solution. With my background in Health and Illustration, I offer a unique perspective when tackling any problem.

Outside of Design, you can find me on a food adventure, weightlifting, sketching on my iPad, or napping.
